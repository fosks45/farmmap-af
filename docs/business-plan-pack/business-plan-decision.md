## Human Approval Requirements

**This gate requires human (founder) approval before the architecture phase begins.**

The founder must confirm:

1. **Personal capital commitment:** Willingness to commit £5,000 personal capital
   to cover Year 1 operating costs on the bootstrap plan.

2. **Year 2 salary deferral:** Acceptance that the base case requires a Year 2 founder
   salary of £40,000 that cannot be funded from Year 2 revenue alone without either
   external funding or personal savings.

3. **Innovate UK application:** Decision on whether to pursue the Innovate UK SMART
   grant application in Q2 Year 1 (4–6 week time investment; non-dilutive if successful).

4. **FRA and yourhonestybox.com partnership:** Commitment to pursue both partnership
   agreements as primary commercial priorities before V1 launch, as stated in the
   go-to-market summary.

5. **3% downside trigger:** Acknowledgement that if Bronze conversion is at or below
   3% by Month 9, the angel funding evaluation is triggered immediately.

An agent may not approve any of these commitments. The founder's confirmation is
required before the architecture phase is commissioned.
